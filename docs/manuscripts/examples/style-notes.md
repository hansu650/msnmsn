## Reusable layout observations

- The title and anonymous author area span both columns on the first page.
- `Abstract---` and `Index Terms---` appear before the first numbered section.
- Body text is compact, justified, and set in two columns with standard IEEE
  margins and section hierarchy.
- First-level headings use Roman numerals; second-level headings use lettered
  subsections; lower levels may use numbered run-in headings.
- Table captions appear above tables. References use bracketed numeric labels
  in two columns and do not require a separate page.
- A conclusion may be followed immediately by references in the remaining
  column space.

## Publication-only elements to exclude

The inspected PDFs contain production metadata that must not appear in the
anonymous review source: conference headers, DOI strips, copyright notices,
continuous page numbers, and IEEE Xplore download watermarks. The manuscript
must also omit author-identifying information during double-blind review.
